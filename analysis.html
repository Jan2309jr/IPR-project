CTD Profiles

A CTD profile is a vertical profile of water parameters, such as conductivity, temperature, and depth, collected by a Conductivity, Temperature, and Depth (CTD) package

T-S Diagram

A graph that plots potential temperature on the vertical axis and salinity on the horizontal axis. T-S diagrams are used to identify water masses, and often include contours of constant potential density, called isopycnals
